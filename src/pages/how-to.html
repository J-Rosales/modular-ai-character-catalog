How to use the catalogue

Follow these steps to discover and export the character you want.

Step 1: Explore featured bots

Start on the landing page to see the current featured selection.

Step 2: Browse the full library

Use the Browse page to scan all available bots and their tags.

Step 3: Open a character

Each character view lists provenance details, modules, and export-ready content.

Step 4: Download bundles

Choose the export format that matches your workflow.
